Regular polyhedra Geometry

This is an experiment to procedurally generate regular polyhedra. Points are spread evenly around a sphere and then interconnected with each other into a complete graph. The resulting lines are sorted and colored by length. Note, this project only generated 3 of the 5 Platonic solids (a limitation of 3D vectors, possibly solved by using arcs across a sphere).
